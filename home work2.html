Nicki Minaj

Bio

Tanya Maraj-Petty, known professionally as Nicki Minaj, is a Trinidadian-born rapper, singer-songwriter, and actress. Dubbed as the "Queen of Rap" by several media outlets, Minaj is one of the most influential and best selling female artists of all time, with 137 million records sold worldwide.
